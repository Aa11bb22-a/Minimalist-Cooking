About Me

I am Aarzoo Savaliya

All things food, culture and travel

Hi there! I'm Aarzop, the creator of "The Mindful and the Minimalist." My journey into the world of minimalist living began when I noticed how the anxiety-inducing clutter of daily decisions was overshadowing the joy of everyday experiences. Seeking simplicity, I found solace in the kitchen, where the act of cooking became my meditation and a testament to the minimalist philosophy.

With each ingredient I choose and every dish I prepare, I aim to emphasize the importance of quality over quantity. My approach to minimalist cooking is not just about creating delectable meals with fewer ingredients; it's about making space for more meaningful engagement with the food we eat and the world around us.

Beyond the kitchen, my passion for minimalism extends to my love for travel. Exploring different cultures and cuisines has allowed me to weave a tapestry of experiences that inform my cooking and lifestyle. Each journey is an opportunity to discover new flavors, techniques, and perspectives, which I bring back to my kitchen and share through my recipes.

Join me as I continue to explore the art of minimalist cooking and the adventure of travel. Together, let's discover how less can indeed be more.

Wishing you joyful simplicity,
Aarzoo Savaliya
